Being social

Adding share buttons

This is a no JavaScript solution.

  • Email mailto:?subject=Email title&body=Email%20message%20including%20the%20link
  • Facebook https://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u=http://www.website.com
  • Google+ https://plus.google.com/share?url=http://www.website.com
  • LinkedIn http://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?mini=true&url=http://www.website.com
  • Twitter https://twitter.com/share?url=http://www.website.com&text=Page%20title
